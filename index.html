The Calvin and Hobbes Voter is a website designed to provide a fun, interactive way to view comic strips from the beloved series Calvin and Hobbes. Strips are presented two at a time. By either pressing the “This comic wins!” button tied to a given comic, or pressing the image of the preferred comic itself, you can vote on the better strip. As a community, we can find out which strips are the best!
